ABOUT US





Who we are:

JQCS provides quality control inspections, factory audits, supplier evaluations, consumer product testing, production control and management, and quality control consulting throughout Somali Region.

It has been said that trust is hard to earn and easy to lose. This has never been more true, than in today’s consumer goods market. When a consumer buys your product, a promise has been made in terms of form, function, and usability. Keeping that promise is the cornerstone to your success. With product recalls and compromised quality at historically high levels, JQCS can help you stand out from the crowd by making sure to keep that promise through our comprehensive quality control solutions. Recognized in the industry for our best in class training and robust ethical standards, JQCS quality assurance, testing, and inspection solutions deliver you the peace of mind and confidence that your products meet your exacting quality standards and align with customer expectations.

OUR MISSION:

The Mission of the Quality Control Company is to facilitate trade of quality Agricultural Produce on both the domestic and international markets through the provision of World-Class Inspection Services.





DIVISION:

JQCS is made up of four business specific divisions and regions.

Inspections – Quality Control Inspections, Testing, and Consulting company.

Testing – Consumer products testing lab based in Jubaland. We perform chemical, functional, safety and packaging testing for consumer goods at our

Sorting Inspections – Doing piece by piece sorting inspections, metal detection and repackaging for customers who manufacture high-end products that require zero defects. We operate our own facilities in Jubaland. We can also provide in factory services.

JDomestic Market – exclusively focused on providing quality control services for Somali manufacturers, suppliers and buyers. We support the growing demand for quality products in the Somali market with the technical services to assist manufacturers in delivering quality procedures.

OBJECTIVES:

Prevent the exportation of inferior or infested products to any destination in the world.

Inspect, sample, grade, seal and disinfest produce for Licensed Buying Companies.

Ensure that facilities of licensed buying companies conform to laid down regulations in order to maintain quality of produce in their custody.

Monitor the operations of Licensed Buying Companies with regard to the safety of produce at all times.

Undertake commercial disinfestations services.
